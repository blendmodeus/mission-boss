Regional CU Stops the Deposit Drain

How a $400M credit union recovered $2.4M in timed deposit runoff in 90 days.

The Situation

Liquidity Was Draining — and Nobody Knew Until Month-End

A $400M Midwest credit union was watching its timed deposits mature and walk out the door. The problem was simple: their reporting cycle only reconciled monthly. By the time leadership saw the runoff, it was already a $2.4M problem.

The CEO was making rate decisions based on numbers that were 30 to 45 days old. Regional banks were offering higher CD rates and pulling members away, but without daily visibility, nobody saw it coming.

What Mission BOSS Did

48 Hours to First Insight

Within two days of deploying the Daily Bottom Line, the leadership team could see the exact rate spread and deposit maturity schedule in real time.

Responsive scorecards monitored deposit retention daily. Within 30 days, attrition dropped by 40%.
