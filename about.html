The Mission Behind Mission BOSS

I Didn't Build This Because the World Needed More Dashboards.

I built it because I kept meeting brilliant credit union leaders who were making big decisions based on data that was already a month old. That's not leadership. That's an autopsy.

I've spent over 25 years building companies at the intersection of software, strategy, and execution. I'm an entrepreneur by trade and a teacher by heart. My focus isn't just on tools — it's on helping you get a better Return on Time Invested (ROTI).

Through my work with TeamOnUP, I realized the "bottom line" isn't just a number on a report — it's something you should see every single day. Mission BOSS is the system I wish I'd had 20 years ago.

The Philosophy

Leaders Have to Lead.

I can give you the system. I can coach you through the process. But I won't run your business for you — and you shouldn't want me to.

Systems Over Saviors

I'm not interested in creating dependency. I build systems that make you more effective, not more reliant on me.

ROTI First

Your Return on Time Invested is your most valuable metric. If it doesn't save you time or make you sharper, it shouldn't be on your calendar.

Facts, Not Feelings

Daily data beats monthly gut checks. The best leaders I work with want to see the real numbers every morning.

Documented Discipline

If a decision isn't written down, did it really get made? I believe in building a clear, written trail of what you decided and why.
